About Us

Sip & Style is a modern, carbon-fiber tumbler designed for people who want durability, comfort, and clean style in one product. Its lightweight yet extremely strong carbon-fiber shell features a matte, scratch-resistant finish that keeps it looking premium every day. Inside, advanced insulation maintains your drink’s temperature for hours, whether you need cold water for a full day out or hot coffee during a busy morning. The secure twist lid prevents leaks, and the soft-grip band makes it easy to hold on the move. Sleek, portable, and versatile, Sip & Style fits seamlessly into school, sports, travel, and daily routines — upgrading every sip with style and performance.

Our Story

Legend says Sip & Style wasn’t “made” — it escaped from a secret carbon-fiber laboratory deep under a volcano. Scientists were trying to build the world’s strongest bicycle, but one night, the carbon fiber they were mixing suddenly gained consciousness, spun itself into a cup shape, and yelled, “BRO I JUST WANNA HOLD JUICE.” Shocked, the scientists tried to stop it, but the tumbler slid across the floor at Mach 7, absorbing every liquid in sight like it was training for a hydration Olympics. It then blasted out of the volcano, did a triple backflip over three confused eagles, and landed perfectly on a picnic table. The locals touched it, realized it could keep water cold for 48 hours straight, and declared it their new king. And that, obviously, is how the Sip&Style tumbler came to exist — not manufactured, but born legendary.
